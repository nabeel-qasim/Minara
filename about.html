Product with Purpose
At Minara, we honor the deep connection that Muslims worldwide have with the Adhan, a call that unites us in devotion and tranquility.

Our vision is to renew this sacred tradition with modern solutions that truly resonate with our lifestyles.

We've poured our passion into creating a product that embodies the beauty of our faith, designed to transform and deepen your prayer experience. As we begin this journey, we invite you to join us in rekindling the spirit of prayer in every Muslim home.

“Relieve us with it, O Bilal.”

This is what the Prophet (saw) would say to signal to Bilal that it was time to make the call to prayer.

The Adhan is an invitation to pause, to disconnect from the stress and busyness of life, and to enter into a state of tranquility.
In Poursuit of Khushu’u

Prayer is meant to be a moment of deep connection and single focus. This deep, soul-filling connection, known as khushu’u, can sometimes feel so difficult to attain. It’s a challenge I know many, like me, deal with.

We aspire to get a sense of khushu’u and mindfulness that gives us tranquility.

That's what Minara is all about. It's not just about counting down the rak'ahs. It's about making every prostration count, about filling up on that peaceful, focused feeling that stays with you long after you've said "Salam" to end the prayer.

I want Minara to be that nudge, that reminder of the incredible power of prayer—not just a pause in your day, but a moment that recharges you, realigns you with your values, and reminds you of who you really are and what you're here for.

Join me, and together let’s make our prayer the highlight of our day, every day.

Prayer & Duaa Lamyaa, Founder of Minara
